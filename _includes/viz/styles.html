{%- comment -%} Visualization styles. Include ONCE per page, before any viz include. Everything here is built from theme tokens (var(--color-*), var(--gray-*)) — no hardcoded hex, matching the theme's own law that nothing in _sass hardcodes a colour. Marks are near-monochrome by design, not by omission: the theme's rule is "colour is wayfinding only", and encoding data classes by LIGHTNESS rather than hue is also the strongest accessibility answer, since a lightness ramp survives every form of colour blindness by construction. Only two steps are ever used as readable classes (ink and gray-500, measured ΔE 31 apart); a third (gray-400) appears only in ordered scales where every segment is direct-labelled, and the lightest steps are track, never data. {%- endcomment -%}
