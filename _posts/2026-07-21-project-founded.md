canonical-castles is now part of [Bussetech Software Studio](https://bussetech.com):
Canonical Castles — how many castles are there? A source-cited dataset that answers per definition, not in general: every structure records which definitions of castle it satisfies, and every definition carries its own count and closure rule.

This post is the site's first feed item — the studio portal aggregates
`/feed.json` from every project site, so publishing a post here is how the
project surfaces on the portal's "latest across the studio" stream.
